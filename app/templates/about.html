AI Agent Capabilities Framework

Agentic Canvas demonstrates 5 industrial AI agent implementations across different tiers of the AI Agent Capabilities Periodic Table (AIA CPT).

    Real-time emissions optimization with reinforcement learning
  • Demo 2: GridMind AI - T4 Multi-Agent System

    EV charging network optimization across India

    R&D acceleration with formulation recommendations
